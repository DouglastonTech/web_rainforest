MAMMALS
Did you  know that there are more than 400 mammal species?
Jaguar
They live in Central and South America.
 They're carnivores.
They grow to be from 5.5 ft-8 ft.
Sloth
They live in Central and South America. They eat leaves from trees . They spend most of their time in trees . They grow to be from 23 to 27 inches.
Okapi
Okapi's are located in Congo,Africa.  They eat buds,fungi,ferns,grasses and leaves . They grow to be from 4.9-6.6.
